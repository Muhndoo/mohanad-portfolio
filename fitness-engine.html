The Brief
A derelict warehouse, converted into an indoor soccer training center for a sports brand — reception, a VR training room, an indoor five-a-side field, a spectator lounge, and a second-floor office and café. The brief called for a space that felt like a professional sports facility, not a converted shed: controlled lighting, a real spectator experience, and a brand identity built into the architecture itself.
